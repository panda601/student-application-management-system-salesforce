# Project Overview: Student Application Management System (SAMS)

SAMS is an enterprise-grade Salesforce-based application designed to manage the end-to-end student enrollment lifecycle, application workflows, payment transactions, and administrative approvals.

## The Business Problem
Universities and educational institutions frequently face bottlenecks in their admissions processes:
*   **Manual Onboarding**: Admissions staff manually enter student biographical info from physical forms, resulting in transcription errors and slow cycle times.
*   **Payment Reconciliation Bottlenecks**: Financial staff struggle to reconcile tuition deposits with specific application files, leading to students being enrolled without making required payments or delays in class starts.
*   **Complex Approvals**: Managers manually audit files for missing transcripts, passport copies, and English proficiency certifications, leading to bottlenecks and lost applicant interest.
*   **Lack of Tracking**: Applicants and staff lack clear visibility into the application evaluation stage.

## The Solution
SAMS automates these processes by providing a unified workspace:
1.  **Unified Registration Wizard**: Combines student profile creation, course selection, and initial deposit collection into a single, atomic multi-step wizard.
2.  **Financial Integrity Engine**: Automatically checks tuition balance in real-time, enforcing the policy that no application is approved until the outstanding balance is zero.
3.  **Einstein AI Insights Panel**: Analyzes applicant risk metrics and recommends actions (like collecting visa copies for international students) to streamline manual reviews.
4.  **Automatic Status Sync & Alerts**: Automates student record activation and triggers transactional email updates.

## Key Outcomes & Metrics
*   **Reduced Processing Time**: Cut application-to-approval turnaround times from weeks to minutes.
*   **Enhanced Revenue Compliance**: Prevented premature approvals, ensuring 100% tuition deposit compliance.
*   **Increased Visibility**: Real-time dashboards provide managers with total pipeline revenue, status splits, and outstanding balances.
